## Company, Mission & Studio

Soft Story Corp is a Korea-based voice studio specializing in Korean localization and dubbing, voice actor recording, and AI voices. The studio carries the emotion, nuance, and intent of content into voice — combining professional voice actors' performance with AI technology — so every line resonates with audiences as if it were made for them. With its own professional recording facilities, Soft Story delivers end-to-end production from translation to final mix, and its expertise spans full-human dubbing, hybrid AI+human localization, and fully AI voice synthesis.

Soft Story Corp's mission rests on a clear belief: AI should empower artists, not replace them. This philosophy takes shape in the Artist AI Voice service, which lets voice actors create their own AI voice. Artists open new revenue streams and global opportunities while retaining full ownership and control — and the artist always comes first.

Soft Story Corp's recording facilities are an in-house studio located on Korean soil. ## Hybrid Dubbing (AI + Human)

Hybrid Localization is Soft Story Corp's middle dubbing tier, positioned between full-human production and fully AI voices. We use AI across every stage of the pipeline — translation drafts, voice generation, timing, and editing — while our Korean directors and language experts guide, correct, and quality-check the result. This delivers most of the polish of human production at a fraction of the cost and timeline.

The defining advantage of Hybrid Localization is its cost and time savings. It cuts roughly 60% on cost and schedule versus premium (full-human) dubbing, so you can do more within the same budget, or the same volume far faster. It is especially well suited to high-volume work, tight budgets, and fast production cycles.

In Hybrid Localization, the division between what AI accelerates and what humans stay in charge of is clear. AI accelerates first-pass translation, voice generation, alignment and timing, and bulk editing. Humans own cultural adaptation, casting and tone direction, performance review, and final QA sign-off. You also decide the bar: we tune how much human pass-over each project gets, so cost scales with the quality you need.

On the common question of whether quality is lower, there is a small step down compared with a full native cast. The final nuance and performance range sit slightly below a full-human cast. But because human directors guide and QA every stage, the result is a world apart from cheap, raw AI dubbing.

Hybrid Localization is best for high-volume game dialogue and live-service content updates, e-learning, corporate, and explainer libraries, YouTube and creator content localized at scale, and projects with fixed budgets or aggressive timelines. By contrast, when you need the final nuance only a human cast can deliver — such as hero characters and key emotional scenes — we recommend premium Korean Localization & Dubbing, and when you need maximum scale and speed, we recommend fully AI Voiceover (Fineapple Voice).

For a hybrid estimate, send us your scope and we'll quote the hybrid option against full-human so you can compare. If you're not sure which tier fits, tell us your project and we'll recommend the right one.

## TTS Dataset Creation & Licensing

Soft Story Corp's TTS Dataset Creation & Licensing service provides high-quality voice datasets to train TTS and AI voice models, handling the full process from recording to curation to licensed delivery. On the principle that training a great TTS or voice model starts with great data, the team designs recording scripts, casts and records professional speakers, then cleans, labels, and segments the audio into a model-ready dataset, delivered with the rights and consent paperwork in order.

What's in a Soft Story Corp TTS dataset starts with multi-speaker or single-speaker corpora recorded in their own studio. Scripts are phonetically balanced to cover your domain, with emotion, style, and speaking-rate variation available on request. The delivered audio is clean, aligned, and labeled, including transcripts, segmentation, and metadata. Datasets can be built in Korean and other languages.

Soft Story Corp's TTS datasets support a range of voice AI applications. Typical use cases include training and fine-tuning TTS and voice-cloning models, speech recognition (ASR) and voice AI research, building branded or character voices, and academic and enterprise R&D.

Rights and consent for Soft Story Corp's TTS datasets are handled clearly. Every speaker records under clear consent, and each dataset comes with licensing terms that fit your use. Terms can be exclusive or non-exclusive, with usage scope defined upfront. There is no scraped data and no ambiguity in the rights.

For a custom voice dataset, you can share your spec, including languages, speakers, hours, and target model, and Soft Story Corp will scope the project. Use the Request a Quote option to start the conversation.
